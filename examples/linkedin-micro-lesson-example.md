# LinkedIn micro-lesson example

```text
The best CTA I saw this week was not a button.

It was a sentence above the button.

“See how much revenue is leaking before you change anything.”

That line does three jobs.

It names the pain.
It lowers the commitment.
It makes the next click feel useful, not risky.

Most CTAs try to force action.
Good CTAs make action feel obvious.
```

- small observation
