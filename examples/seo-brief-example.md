# SEO brief example

## Keyword

```text
cahier de vacances maths
```

## Intent

Commercial and informational. Parents want a way to keep skills warm during summer.

## Page type

Guide plus offer pathway.

## SERP angle

Most pages list exercises or sell generic workbooks. Opportunity: explain how to choose the right level and avoid wasting the summer on random worksheets.

## CTA mapping

- Early soft CTA: diagnostic quiz
- Mid-page CTA: summer revision plan
- Final CTA: course or tutoring offer

## Proof to gather

- GSC seasonal query data
- Google Trends summer spike
- GA4 engagement on existing rentrée articles
- conversion data for diagnostic/course pages

## Measurement plan

- 7 days: indexation and tracking
- 28 days: GSC impressions/clicks and GA4 engagement
- 60 days: conversion path performance
