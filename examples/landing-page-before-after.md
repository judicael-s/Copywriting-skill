# Landing page before and after

## Context

A SaaS tool helps support teams close tickets faster.

## Before

```text
Streamline your customer support workflow with our innovative AI-powered platform.
```

## Diagnosis

- Too generic.
- No reader.
- No proof.
- No concrete outcome.
- Could belong to 200 tools.

## After

```text
Close repeat support tickets before they reach the queue.

Acme reads past conversations, drafts the first answer, and shows your team what to check before they reply.

Support teams use it to cut handoffs, answer faster, and keep customers from repeating the same problem twice.
```

## Why it works

- The first line names a specific pain.
- The mechanism is visible.
- The benefit is operational, not abstract.
- The copy gives the reader a scene they recognize.
