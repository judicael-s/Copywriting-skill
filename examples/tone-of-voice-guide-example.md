# Tone of voice guide example

## Voice summary

Clear, useful, commercially sharp.

The brand sounds like an operator who has done the work, not a motivational account.

## We are

- specific
- calm
- practical
- honest about limits
- focused on outcomes

## We are not

- hype-driven
- vague
- over-polished
- full of buzzwords
- clever at the cost of clarity

## Sentence style

Short when the idea is simple.
Longer when the reasoning earns it.

No fake urgency.
No generic encouragement.
No announced conclusions.

## Example

Weak:

```text
Unlock the power of a comprehensive organic growth strategy.
```

Strong:

```text
Turn search demand into pages, links, and offers people act on.
```
